Vicc

Virtualised Infrastructure for Cloud Computing.

This course presents the virtualized infrastructures that are at the core of today cloud environment with a focus on solutions used to virtualize the computing infrastructure

We discuss some of the challenges that occur today at large scale such as providing security, quality of service and reducing the total ownership costs. We then present and test present and ongoing solutions.

Pitching the course
